Chicken with Vinegar (AKA Tarragon Chicken) and a side of Slaw (different variation)

I followed the recipes in the book "Salt Fat Acid Heat" by Samin Nosrat. The linked videos are for reference.

  1. I start by setting up the ingredients for the Slaw:
  2. Slice the cabbage crosswise and season with salt and soy sauce.
  3. Slice the onions and macerate them in lime juice.
  4. Chop some roasted peanuts.
  5. Add some finely grated ginger, a garlic clove, some scallions, and Rice Vinegar and we get our Slaw.

Next, let's make the main event

  1. Set up the ingredients
  2. Cover the chicken in all-purpose flour

  4. I sauteed the chicken while slicing the onions and removed them once they're brown on both sides
  5. Next, we cook the onions with butter until brown
