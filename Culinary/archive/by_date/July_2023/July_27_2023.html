Slow-cooked Salmon with Great Northen Beans and Macerated Onions

  1. Set up the ingredients
  2. Start by letting the beans simmer and letting the rice cook.
    Meanwhile, we skin the Salmon.
  3. Make a bed of herbs for the salmon to cook on (Tarragon and Bay leaves).
  4. Next, rub the Salmon with Olive oil and season with salt. I spread some lemon slices on top for extra flavor.

    5. Let it cook in the oven for 40 minutes at 300 degrees fahrenheit.

  5. As the salmon cooked, I made an avocado salad
  6. Soon enough, the salmon is done
  7. Lastly, we put it all together

P.S. macerated onions are underrated!
