Greek Salad and Chicken with Lentil Rice (Adas Polo o Morgh)

For the Greek Salad:

  1. Set up the ingredients
  2. Stripey peel the cucumbers and slice them 1/2 inch thick
  3. Add in the Cherry tomatos and diced garlic
  4. Next add the black pitted olives
  5. Then add feta cheese and the macerated onions
  6. Lastly add Oil and Vinegar and toss the salad around

For the Chicken with Lentil Rice

  1. Set up the ingredients
  2. Set Lentils to cook in simmering water
  3. In high heat, brown the chicken on each side slightly and set it aside
  4. Drain the pan and melt 3 tbs of butter on it
  5. Once the butter is melted, put the sliced onion in the pan
  6. Next spread around a tbs of grinded cumin on the onions
  7. Put a couple Bay Leaves on top too
  8. Once the onions have browned/caramelized, it's time to pour in the rice, dates, and raisins
  9. Let the rice brown a little, then add in the cooked lentils and pour enough water (4 cups)
  10. Lastly, place the chicken on top and let it all steam for about 40 minutes.
