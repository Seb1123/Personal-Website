Cacio e Pepe

  1. Set up your ingredients
  2. Boil the pasta for 6-7 minutes
  3. Drain the pasta but save some pasta water for later
  4. Heat up some coarsley crushed black peppercorns in olive oil
  5. Once the smell of black peppercorns starts to fill the air, drop the pasta into the pan
    Coat the pasta in the olive oil and black peppercorns.
  6. Next pour in the pasta water and after a 2 to 3 minutes sprinkle the grated parmesan
    slowly and in small portions so that it combines with the pasta water to make somewhat of an emulsion (this is the hardest part)
  7. Keep pouring parmesan and pasta water until you get the amount and consistency you like
