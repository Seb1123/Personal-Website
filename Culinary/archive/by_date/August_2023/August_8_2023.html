Chocolate Midnight Cake

  1. First, gather the ingredients
  2. Mix Heavy Cream and a little vanilla extract
  3. Whisk the Heavy Cream with Vanilla and add granulated sugar
  4. Next, mix in all the dry ingredients together: Flour, Salt, Sugar, Cocoa, and Baking Soda
  5. On a separate bowl, mix in the wet ingredients: Canola Oil, Vanilla extract, and boiling water
  6. Now pour in the wet ingredients to mix it in the dry ingredients' bowl
  7. Don't forget to pour the eggs in the mix. I almost forgot, oops.
  8. Then, preheat the oven to 350 Fahrenheit and pour the mix into two 8-inch cake ring molds
  9. After 30 minutes in the oven, the cake is ready to be layered.

    10. Now use the frosting from the beginning (kept chilling in the freezer)
