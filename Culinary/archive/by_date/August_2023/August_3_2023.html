Pasta Alfredo

  1. Set up the ingredients
  2. Start reducing the Heavy Cream
  3. Meanwhile, make the pasta
  4. Eventually the cream will be reduced significantly
  5. On a separate pan, melt 3 tbp of butter
  6. Once melted add in the minced garlic
  7. Next, add the reduced cream
  8. Then, add the Fettuccine
  9. Once you coat the Fettuccine in the cream, start adding the grated parmesan and pasta water until you get the consistency you wish

Now we put it on a plate and sprinkle some black peppercorn
