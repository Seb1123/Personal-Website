Breaded Chicken Cutlets

  1. Melt two sticks of unsalted butter on low heat.
    Remove the build-up at the surface of the butter to be left with only the clarified butter.
  2. Set up your breading station: (flour, eggs, and Panko)

    3. Excited to try this Panko!

  3. Next tenderize the chicken thighs to make them more even-leveled for more efficient cooking.
  4. Then drop the chicken in the flour, soak them in the eggs, and cover them with crumbs.
  5. Set the stove to medium-high heat and pour in the clarified butter.
    When the butter starts to sizzle drop in the Chicken.

    6. Turn the chicken thigh every 2 minutes until thoroughly cooked.
