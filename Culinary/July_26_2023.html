Avocado Salad with Strip Steak

This meal is straight-forward and delicious!

  1. Set up the ingredients for the salad: Red Onions, Tomatoes, Cucumber, Orange (or another citrus), and Avocado.

    2. Macerate the onions, slice the cucumbers, dice the tomatoes, peel the orange, and spoon chunks off of the avocado.

  2. After plating the salad, it's time to cook the steak.

    3. After cooking the steak for 10 minutes on high heat, baste it with butter on low heat for another 6 minutes.
    Let it cool and then slice it into strips to place on top of the salad.
